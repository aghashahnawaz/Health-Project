Who Is Only Health

We’re a consumer health marketing agency whose sole purpose is to build trusted brands
that impact the nation’s health and wellbeing.

Bundles

Rather than offer standalone health
marketing services, we bundle PR,
digital, social content together to
empower health brands to make a
bigger impact.

Nationwide

Headquarter in London, we have 7
key offices across UK and partners
in more than 100 countries around the world. We think global but act local.

In-Board-Staff

If you want to grow your in-house health marketing team but like the flexibility of external support, we’ll inboard PR, digital, social and content experts into your offices.

Rep-Managment

In healthcare, reputation is everything. We enhance, defend and protect the reputation of some of the world’s most respected healthcare brands.


Integrated Healthcare Marketing

People claim that all health communications agencies are the
same. We’re not!

It goes without saying that we have all the traditional marketing
bases covered…but that’s where tradition stops.

There is no guess work in what we do. We base all our health
marketing and communications campaigns on painstaking
research and thorough data analysis to pinpoint target audiences
and identify the most effective way to reach them.

Our strategies don’t rely on PR or advertising or social outreach.
Our campaigns are integrated to maximise the impact by blending
the full pallet of colours from the health marketing and
communications paint box.

We embrace the latest technologies to reach the only place that
matters – the space between the ears of consumers, patients and
HCPs.
